## Key Design Patterns

### Real-Time Elements
- **Live Badges**: Red "LIVE" indicator (universal across all platforms)
- **Viewer Count**: Displayed in top corner with eye icon ğŸ‘ï¸ or number
- **Flowing Chat**: Comments scroll bottom-to-top or left side
- **Animated Reactions**: Hearts, emojis floating across screen
- **Purchase Notifications**: Toast/popup when viewers buy ("John just bought 2x Product!")

### Urgency/Scarcity
- **Countdown Timers**: Flash sales, limited offers
- **Stock Indicators**: "Only 3 left!" warnings
- **Flash Discounts**: Available only during live
- **First Buyer Badges**: Recognition for early purchasers
- **Live-Only Prices**: Special pricing only during broadcast

### Seamless Commerce
- **Persistent Cart**: Accessible from any screen via icon
- **Picture-in-Picture**: Browse products without leaving stream
- **One-Tap Checkout**: Saved payment/shipping info
- **No App Switching**: Complete purchase flow in-app
- **Instant Add to Cart**: Single tap from live stream
